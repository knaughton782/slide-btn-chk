A state-mandated standardized test where a student scores in a certain percentile

Incorrect. If the test were criterion-referenced, every student could score in the 99th percentile. However, since percentiles rank students by achievement, only the top 1% can score in the 99th percentile.
Correct. Students' scores are compared with others and the percentile is their percentage score above the norm group.
