Advertising Quote: "Bayer aspirin protects against heart attacks."

Background: The FTC required Bayer to launch a consumer education campaign because the claim that a daily regiment of aspirin was unsubstantiated.

Failure to Disclose

Incorrect. Bayer disclosed everything to the consumer.

Flawed & Insignificant Research

Correct! Bayer's claims were found to be unsupported by the accepted authorities.

Product Disparagement

Incorrect. Bayer did not discredit a competitor's product.
